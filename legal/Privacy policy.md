# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: 21.07.2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Macro Tracker collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our application.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you create an account and use Macro Tracker, we collect:

- **Account information**: email address and password (used for authentication)
- **Profile information**: name, date of birth, gender, height, and activity level (used for BMR and TDEE calculations)
- **Weight logs**: weight entries you record over time
- **Food and meal data**: meals you log, including estimated calories and macronutrients (protein, carbs, fats)
- **Usage data**: app interactions necessary to provide core features (e.g. streaks, daily totals)

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide to:

- Create and maintain your account
- Calculate your personalized calorie and macronutrient targets
- Track your progress over time (weight history, meal logs, streaks)
- Send optional reminder notifications, if enabled
- Improve and maintain the app's functionality

## 3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or loss.

## 4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share your data with third parties except:

- When required by law
- With service providers who help us operate the app (e.g. our database and authentication provider), under confidentiality obligations
- With Gemini AI, to estimate calories and macronutrients for meals you describe. This includes the meal description you submit, your daily calorie and macro targets, and your nutrition totals so far that day. We currently use Google's free API tier, meaning Google may use this content to improve its products, and it may be reviewed by human reviewers, per Google's terms. If you are located in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, Google's paid-tier data terms apply to you regardless of tier.

## 5. Notifications

If you enable notifications, we use this permission solely to send you reminders related to logging your meals. You can disable notifications at any time from the app's Settings.

## 6. Your Rights

You may:

- Update your profile information at any time within the app
- Request deletion of your account, which schedules your data for removal
- Contact us with questions about your data at costel.daniel.sbirna@gmail.com

## 7. Account Deletion

When you request account deletion, your account is flagged for deletion and your data will be permanently removed within 30 days, in accordance with applicable law.

## 8. Children's Privacy

Macro Tracker is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect information from children under this age.

## 9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
